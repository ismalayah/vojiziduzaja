What is Millie Bobby Browns net worth?

Arriving on the scene in Hollywood as a young star is certainly becoming the norm, and with the entire cast of Stranger Things reaching new heights and blossoming in their own individual careers, it hardly comes as a surprise that a large portion of followers and fans are interested to learn more about the kids-turned-adults outside of the series.

More specifically, interest has bloomed around Millie Bobby Brown, who has quickly risen up the celebrity ranks and cemented herself as one of the world’s most popular young actresses. This stunning rise to stardom has led to many people questioning exactly how much the star is worth, and how much she has accumulated thus far over her ever-growing career.

Millie Bobby Brown’s net worth

According to the website Celebrity Net Worth, Brown is currently worth $14 million. However, this number will likely quickly rise as her fame grows and she makes her presence felt on new projects. This is doubly true if her upcoming films do well at the box office. In the past, Brown, along with her fellow younger cast mates from Stranger Things, all made $30,000 per episode in the show’s first two seasons. From there, the cast was given a pay increase due to the series’ overwhelming popularity, with Brown making close to $2 million per season now.

This success will likely lead to Brown becoming a hot property in Hollywood, leading to her making even more money for both her acting roles and her future modeling appearances. So, it’s clear that her millions show no signs of slowing down, and if anything, her net worth is only set to increase.

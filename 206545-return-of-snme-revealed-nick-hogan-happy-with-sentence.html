Return Of SNME Revealed, Nick Hogan Happy With Sentence?


It has been announced that a WWE Saturday Night’s Main Event will air as a one hour special at 9:00 p.m. on Saturday, August 2 on NBC. The last time they taped Saturday Night’s Main Event, it was before the previous edition of Raw, so more than likely, the one-hour special will be taped before the live Raw show on July 28, 2008, which is taking place at the Verizon Center in our Nation’s Capital, Washington D.C.

According to WWE.com, on Friday, June 20 from 3-6 p.m., WWE Diva Ashley Massaro will be signing autographs at Camden Yards Sports Complex in Baltimore, Maryland.

Just minutes after Nick Hogan was sentenced to 8 months in jail, his lawyer Kevin Hayslett spoke to reporters outside of the Pinellas County courthouse in Florida. Hayslett said Nick is “happy with the sentence” and wants “resolution out of respect for his friend John.” Hayslett also the media that Nick was not a convicted felon and would go on “to do good things with his life.”
